Here’s a timeline of Musk’s most notable business endeavors—both successful and not—over the past three decades.

Elon Reeve Musk FRS ( born June 28, 1971) is a business magnate and investor. He is the founder, CEO, and Chief Engineer at SpaceX; angel investor, CEO, and Product Architect of Tesla, Inc.; founder of The Boring Company; and co-founder of Neuralink and OpenAI.

  • 1995-Along with his brother,Musk founded Zip2
  • 1999-Musk invested some of his windfall from the Zip2 sale into his next venture: X.com, an online bank.
  • 2000-X.com merged with software company Confinity just one year later, forming PayPal
  • 2002-Musk next set his sights on a lofty goal: space exploration, and the future colonization Mars.
  • 2004-Musk is perhaps best known for his leadership at Tesla, the electric car company named after famed inventor Nikola Tesla.
  • 2015-Musk co-founded OpenAI as a non-profit in 2015.
  • 2016-The Boring Company,the Boring Company is one of Musk’s side projects.
  • 2022-After buying up enough stock to make him a majority shareholder
